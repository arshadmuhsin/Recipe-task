Thandai Ice Cream By Chef Arshad

Ingredients:

  • Cream ½ kg
  • Milk 1 liter
  • Green cardamom powder 6-8 pieces
  • Cashew nut powder ¼ cup
  • Pistachio powder 2 tbsp
  • Saffron a pinch
  • Almond powder 3 tbsp
  • Whole dried milk 1 cup
  • Corn flour 5 tbsp
  • Evaporated milk 1 tin

    • Method:

    1. In a pan add milk, cream and sugar.
    2. Bring it to boil then add evaporated milk mixed with corn flour and keep mixing for a minute.
    3. Now add remaining ingredients and cook for 5 minutes then cool and grind to a fine paste.
    4. Then add this to the ice cream machine and freeze for 45 minutes.
    5. Remove and freeze. Serve.
